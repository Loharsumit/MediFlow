## 🚀 Overview

**MediFlow ERP** is a full-stack, web-based Pharmacy Management System designed to modernize traditional medical shop operations. It centralizes inventory, billing, customer management, and reporting into a single efficient platform.

---

## ✨ Core Features

🔹 **Inventory Management** – Real-time tracking of medicines and stock levels  
🔹 **Point of Sale (POS)** – Fast and accurate billing system  
🔹 **Customer & Supplier Management** – Organized record keeping  
🔹 **Dashboard & Analytics** – Business insights and performance tracking  
🔹 **AI Invoice Scanning** – Automated data extraction from invoices
